- **Product & Production:** I architected and deployed Axle 4, a full hardware-software onboard compute platform for autonomous drone operations, driving the project from initial concept to scaled production deployment in under six months. I also managed a cross-functional team of 10 engineers specializing in collaborative autonomy, model-based reinforcement learning, and Bayesian state estimation.
- **Advanced Research:** I have led the technical execution for multi-agent autonomous systems across high-priority DARPA, USAF, ARL, and US Army programs, securing over $3M in SBIR funding, including a Phase III award dedicated to transitioning experimental architectures into fielded systems.
